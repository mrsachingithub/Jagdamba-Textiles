About Jagdamba Textiles

Welcome to Jagdamba Textiles, your premier destination for high-quality fabrics in the heart of Surat’s textile hub. Experience the blend of tradition and modernity with our exquisite collection of fabrics.

Our Legacy

Located in the iconic New Pashupati Market, we have been serving the textile industry for years, providing premium Georgette, Velvet, Satin, and various other fabrics to designers and manufacturers across the country.

Our Quality

We pride ourselves on the superior quality of our textiles. Every meter of fabric is checked for consistency, color fastness, and texture to ensure our customers receive only the best.
